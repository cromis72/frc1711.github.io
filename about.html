Team 1711 is an FIRST Robotics Team based in Traverse City, Michigan. This website is built and maintained by the programmers on this team, and hosted by Github. This website is fully open source, as is all of the code used on the robot. You can find our team's website here, and we're also on Facebook and Instagram. We also have an app on the app store.
